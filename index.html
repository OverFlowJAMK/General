About our company

We are going to build a Software Defined Network to Challenge Factory 2016, which is a summer project organized by Jyväskylä University of Applied Sciences. The company has three employees. The network is planned to connect three classrooms in school. Those three classrooms are a project room of a company named KUMOS, a general working room and a room for SDN- operator.
